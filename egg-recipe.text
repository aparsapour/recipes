Ingredients:
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/4 cup vegetable oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
 1. Preheat pan to warm. add oil
 
 2. Break the egg shells & drop it gently onto the hot oil

 3. wait till it gets white & scrable all the egg for a time of 1 minutes.

 4. Turn fire off & pour the egg into your dish to serve.
